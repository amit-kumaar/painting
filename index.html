About our website

The author proposes to develop a website that would ease out the struggles that artist have to face in order to showcase their talent and interact with their clients. This would also help the interested patrons to easily approach the meister in instant. The system would ease the communication between both parties through the digital platform. In our existing system, no such website is developed for the artists to sell their sketches. Presently the artist manually describe about their work. With this website, artists will get a platform and their art will get and exposure in addition this will ease their customers too.
