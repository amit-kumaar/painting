Shipping & packaging

Shipping in Ludhiana
We offer free shipping for both rolled and framed paintings in ludhiana. We only work with reputed courier partners for domestic deliveries. Delivery timeframe is mentioned on each painting page. Some locations which are remote may attract special delivery charges. Our customer care will connect to you in this case once order is placed.



Packaging
We process military grade packaging for all our shipments, so that they could absorb jerks and stack weight in the transit, - and reach you in safe and sound condition. Painting rolls are put in hard protective tubes and sealed at ends. We make custom 25mm to 40mm thick walled (depending on size) thermocol boxes for each frame, which is again wrapped with 6-8 layers of double layered bubble sheets. Gift wrapping is also available on request for free. In rare case, if any of the artwork is lost or damaged in transit - we will happily offer you refunds. If the frame is delivered damaged we will offer full refund of framing charges.
